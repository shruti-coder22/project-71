Castle Hill High School


Truth Is Strenght

Hi!

Established in 1963, Castle Hill High School is a large, coeducational high school set in leafy, well maintained grounds. Academic excellence is prized. It is a school that has a very strong creative and performing arts program and a dynamic sporting tradition. Additionally, the school's student leadership and well being programs are excellent, providing opportunity for students to learn about themselves, work in teams and take initiative. Students and staff are happy, focused and work to support one another in a cooperative environment.
In order to extend and challenge students, there is a commitment to broad extra-curricula activities including an annual musical production, debating, concert bands, public speaking, dance ensembles, chess club and annual overseas study tours.
Facilities are extensive and include, for example, six networked computer laboratories, a multi media centre, a photographic darkroom, a performing arts studio, a language conferencing centre and a gymnasium.Recently refurbished classrooms and Science laboratories add to the school's modern and aesthetic appeal.

The safe and nurturing atmosphere of the school fosters a high standard of student behaviour, marked by self discipline and consideration for others. The school aims to help students become independent learners who have developed the knowledge, skills and confidence necessary to meet the challenges of adult life.

School Achievements

HSC
Castle Hill High School generally performs well in the HSC, with many students achieving band 6s each year. In 2018, 231 were achieved, and one student attained the maximum possible rank in the state for their subject.
Extracurricular
Castle Hill High School can be noted by their strong participation in extracurricular activities, including sport, musical production, leadership, technology and public competitions. Additionally, the school has a high level of participation and commitment to various charitable events, with a high volunteer rate in activities such as the 40 Hour Famine, Relay For life, The Salvation Army Red Shield Door Knock Appeal and the World's Greatest Shave.

Campus Life

  • Library

    Castle Hill High School Library provides both print and digital resources to support the learning of students.
    We are dedicated to providing wonderful print, e-book and audio reading choices to encourage reading for pleasure. This is grounded on research that shows that reading widely is essential for intellectual, personal and social development.
    The two teacher librarians work as information specialists, liaising with staff to provide lessons focusing on specific skills related to research, ICT, critical and creative thinking and literature across the curriculum.

  • Auditorium

    The school auditorium (or hall) is a wonderfull place, who holds a special place in all of our hearts, as the place where all year 7s are initiated into the ranks ao our small country town.
    The walls are adourned with boards and advertisments for our yearly school musical.
